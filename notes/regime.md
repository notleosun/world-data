# Historical FIW Status and Ratings Information

The following worksheets list all country ratings and statuses since the annual **Freedom in the World (FIW)** survey was first compiled to cover the year **1972**.

## Key

- **Survey Edition** refers to the various editions of *Freedom in the World*, except for **Janâ€“Feb 1973 through Janâ€“Feb 1977**, which are from the bimonthly journal *Freedom at Issue*.

- **PR** stands for **Political Rights**  
- **CL** stands for **Civil Liberties**  
- **Status** refers to the **Freedom Status**

PR and CL are measured on a **1â€“7 scale**, where:
- **1** = highest degree of freedom  
- **7** = lowest degree of freedom  

Freedom Status categories:
- **F** = Free  
- **PF** = Partly Free  
- **NF** = Not Free

## Methodology

- **Until 2003**, countries and territories were classified as:
  - **Free**: combined PR/CL average between **1.0 and 2.5**
  - **Partly Free**: between **3.0 and 5.5**
  - **Not Free**: between **5.5 and 7.0**

- **Beginning with the 2003 ratings**, classifications were revised:
  - **Partly Free**: combined average between **3.0 and 5.0**
  - **Not Free**: combined average between **5.5 and 7.0**

- Other methodological changes have been introduced periodically.  
  For detailed explanations of each editionâ€™s methodology and changes, consult the methodology essays published with each edition.
